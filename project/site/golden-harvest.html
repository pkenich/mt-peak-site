The Origin

At the peak of the season, when the tips turn gold, our pickers choose only the very best of the garden.

Golden Harvest is our most generous tea — fully oxidised, deep in colour, honeyed in the cup. Every tip is selected by hand at the height of the season, then carried through a full, careful oxidation to bring out its warmth and depth.

Presented in a hand-finished gift box, it is built to be given as much as brewed. Certified organic, single-origin, and finished at our own factory — no middlemen, only the mountain and the hands that tend it.
