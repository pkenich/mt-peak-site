The Philosophy

Most tea is grown for yield. Ours is grown for altitude — where thin air and cold nights force the leaf to concentrate everything it is into a single, slow, deliberate harvest.

01 — The Land

Where clouds become gardens

In eastern Nepal, our gardens sit at 2,400 metres. The mountains do the work no machine can: mist that slows growth, granite soils that sharpen flavour, and a stillness you can taste in the cup.

02 — The Hands

Plucked by people, not machines

Two leaves and a bud. Each one chosen by hands that have read these slopes for generations. No shortcuts. No volume targets. Only the standard the leaf deserves.

03 — The Proof

Certified, traced, honest

Organic certified and single-origin — every batch traceable to the garden and the season it was picked. Provenance you can verify, not just believe.
